# About me

I'm **Gustavo de Moura** — a Senior Machine Learning Engineer focused on
**computer vision**, **deep learning**, and applied AI R&D. I enjoy taking
research from paper to production: autonomous systems, intelligent agents, and
tools that turn messy real-world data into something useful.

## What I work on

- **Computer vision** — object detection, continual learning, sim-to-real
  domain adaptation, image enhancement.
- **Autonomous systems** — UAV path planning, obstacle avoidance, aerial
  inspection of infrastructure ([Harpia](/publications#harpia)).
- **Intelligent agents** — Monte Carlo methods for continuous-action
  chance-constrained MDPs ([Master's thesis](/publications#master-thesis)).

  neural document enhancement.
